Chef DJ

A fun design concept that combines music and cooking.

Chef DJ gives you a place to save your favorite music and recipes all in one place. Want music suggestions based on your recipies or your shopping list? Let Chef DJ do it.

You can also creat playlists for your favoirte recipes and share them with your friends and family.

Overview

Music and Cooking go together like peas and carrots. Chef DJ give you a place to save your favorite recipes, share them with your firends,get music suggestions and create your favorite playlists.

Objective

To create a bodly different recipe app with a uniqe concept making it stand apart from all the recipe apps out there and to give users a place where they can easliy access food and music together.

Challenge

This project went through many phases and taught me a lot about the importance of keeping the UI as clean and simple as possible so that users can navigate with ease and enjoyment.

Illustrations

These vibrant and bold illustrations elicit positve energy while creating a fun, approachable and user friendly tone. The simplified shapes depicting musical instruments were created to provoke a dramatic and memorable backdrop to complement the simple high angle shots of food.

User Flows

Navigation

After serveral user tests and various iterations, careful consideration of the color, size and placement of UI elements helped to create visual hierarchy that aims to promote usability and allow users to navigate delightfully throughout the app.

A/B Testing

65% of users voted on the stero with the frying pan in the speaker of the radio mostly because they preferred the simplicity of the design. Some also said they preferred it because it felt more on brand with the app than the abstract egg shape on the far right.

The initial screens (on the left) contain backgrounds with sahpes depicting food (meat, apple, egg and avocado) but htese felt less on brand with the app and the users preferred the musical instrument shapes.
