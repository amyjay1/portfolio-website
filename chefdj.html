Chef DJ

A fun design concept that combines music and cooking.

Chef DJ gives you a place to save your favorite music and recipes all in one place. Want music suggestions based on your recipies or your shopping list? Let Chef DJ do it. You can also create playlists for your favorite recipes and share them with your friends and family.

Overview

Music and Cooking go together like peas and carrots. Chef DJ gives you a place to save your favorite recipes, share them with your firends, get music suggestions and create your favorite playlists.

Objective

To create a boldly different recipe app with a unique concept making it stand apart from all the recipe apps out there and to give users a place where they can easliy access music and recipes at the same time.

Challenge

This project went through many phases and taught me a lot about the importance of keeping the UI as clean and simple as possible so that users can navigate with ease and enjoyment.

Illustrations

These vibrant and bold illustrations elicit positive energy while creating a fun, approachable and user friendly tone. The simplified shapes depicting musical instruments were created to provoke a dramatic and memorable backdrop to complement the simple high angle shots of food.

User Flows

Navigation

After serveral user tests and various iterations, careful consideration of the color, size and placement of UI elements helped to create a visual hierarchy that aims to promote usability and allows users to navigate delightfully throughout the app.

A/B Testing

65% of users voted on the radio with the frying pan and egg in its speaker mostly because they preferred the simplicity of the design. Some also said they preferred it because it felt more on brand with the app than the abstract egg shape on the right (below).

The initial screens (below) contain backgrounds with shapes depicting food (a steak, an apple, an egg and an avocado) but these felt less on brand with the app and users preferred the musical instrument shapes (see above).
